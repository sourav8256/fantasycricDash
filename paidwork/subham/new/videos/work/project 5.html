This is one of my best projects for the German region: a video ad for Imodium, a brand of anti-diarrheal medication. I was in charge of the animation part of this project, which involved creating and animating the characters, using the parallax effect to create a sense of depth and movement, and composing the scenes in After Effects. I used the DUIK plugin for the character animation, and I aimed for a stop motion type look to match the style and tone of the ad.

This project was a great opportunity for me to showcase my skills and passion for character animation, 2.5D animation, and composition. I enjoyed every step of the process, from designing the characters to adding the final touches. I also learned a lot from working with a talented team of professionals who provided me with feedback and support. The animation part of this project took me 40 hours to complete, and I am very proud of the result.

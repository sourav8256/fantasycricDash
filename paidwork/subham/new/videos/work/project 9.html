In my leisure time, I derive immense pleasure from crafting original and exceptional motion content through the exploration of diverse after-effects scripts and plugins. One of my most notable personal achievements is this project which involved extensive utilization of various scripts, plugins and ae expressions, requiring a total of 120 hours to achieve the desired animation. This project was assigned to me by an interviewer as a means to evaluate my proficiency. While provided with a rough storyboard and voiceover, I took it upon myself to revamp the storyboard and animate the entire video, complementing it with background music and sound effects for enhanced impact.

The most formidable aspect of this project was the development of a 3D human skeleton in Blender and devising innovative match cuts for a seamless video flow. My attention to detail was crucial as I meticulously crafted every micro animation in the project. To enhance the visual effects, I utilized advanced tools such as Newton 3 plugins, Saber, plexus, deep glow and even wrote custom AE code. This project served as a valuable opportunity for me to expand my expertise in Blender and sound design.

The brief was to showcase the benefits of Neutrogena's Hydro Boost Water cream, a moisturizer that hydrates 9 times and boosts the skin's barrier.

The concept was to use 3D water slashes and a water molecule to illustrate the product's ingredients and effects. The storyboard was done by the other storyboard artist of the team. I used Blender to create the 3D elements such as water droplets, water splashes and used After Effects for the final animation, composition, and color correction. The VO, BGM, SFX was done by the dedicated sound team.

The whole process took me 18 hours to complete, but it was worth it. I enjoyed working on this project because it challenged me to create a minimal design, a clean look and an engaging content.

Thank you for watching!
